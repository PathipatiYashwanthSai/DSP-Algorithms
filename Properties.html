Properties of DFT


All of these properties of the discrete Fourier transform (DFT) are applicable for discrete-time signals that have a DFT. Meaning these properties of DFT apply to any generic signal x(n) for which an X(k) exists. (x(n) X(k)) where X(k)= ∑n=0N-1 x(n) e-2πjkn/N.
